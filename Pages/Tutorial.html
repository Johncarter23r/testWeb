How to use

This is not dynamic. Consider it a simple template. Instructions:

  • Visit the patient page. Click "SEND REQUEST" button and allow the website to know your location.

  • Once the website knows your location, it will send the coordinates to the operator page.

  • Once operator receives the coordinates, he can click on the "View on google maps" hyperlink and it will send him to the google map with the patient's location.

  • The patient can also visit hospital section. In this section the patient will be able to see multiple hospitals near them and the treatment that is offered by those hospital. The patient can make an appointment with the doctor. But it is not implemented yet.

  • The admin page is not implemented yet. It may contain information about the patient that used the medical service of a specific hospital. It will also contain information about the people who work at the hospital
